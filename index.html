Common Questions

Straight
answers.

No fluff. Here's what most folks ask before booking.

What areas does BR Lawn Care serve? +

We serve residential and commercial properties across Eastern North Carolina and surrounding counties. If you're not sure we cover your area, call us at 252.503.0984 and we'll tell you straight.

How much does lawn care cost? +

Lawn care starts at $55. We offer one-time, weekly, and bi-weekly schedules. Every estimate is free and includes a straightforward price with no hidden fees or upsells.

Are you insured? +

Yes — BR Lawn Care is fully insured for residential and commercial work. We can provide a certificate of insurance on request for HOAs, property managers, and commercial clients.

How fast do you reply to estimate requests? +

Within 24 hours, usually same-day. Most jobs can be scheduled in the same week.

What services do you offer beyond mowing? +

Tree and stump removal, land clearing, landscape installs (sod, mulch, rock, plantings), pressure washing, and ongoing property management for rentals, HOAs, and commercial lots.

Do you handle commercial and HOA accounts? +

Yes. We manage commercial lots, rental portfolios, and HOA common areas with consistent schedules, one point of contact, and one monthly invoice.
